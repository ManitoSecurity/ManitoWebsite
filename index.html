AFFORDABLE

Gone are the days of monthly fees, ongoing charges, and costly set up charges. Manito Security is a low, one time purchase with a lifetime of free firmware upgrades.

OPEN SOURCE

Manito Security is made up of a team of tinkerers. We share that itch with thousands of other developers to peer inside. Because of this itch, very early on, we decided that our security system would be developer friendly.

PLUG AND PLAY

Just because Manito Security is open source doesn’t mean you need an engineering degree from MIT to operate our security system. We have made spent many hours to make set up is a breeze and basic customization painless.

WHO WE ARE

Manito Security is born out of Gonzaga University’s Senior Design Experience. With the oversight of Dave James of Avista and Patrick Nowacki of Gonzaga, Manito Security is the product of four engineers in the Class of 2015.
