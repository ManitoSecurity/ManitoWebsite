WHO WE ARE

Manito Security is born out of Gonzaga University’s Senior Design Experience. With the oversight of Dave James of Avista and Patrick Nowacki of Gonzaga, Manito Security is the product of four engineers in the Class of 2015.

Brian Gravelle grew up in Latrobe, Pennsylvania before moving to Spokane, Washington to study Computer Engineering under the tutelage of Gonzaga University's erudite faculty. In addition to his interest in computers Brian also enjoys playing 500 (a bridge-like card game) and taking long walks on the beach.
